Simply Class is a complete apparel source out of India and works in the entire region with particular emphasis on Bangalore / Tirupur / Chennai / Mumbai / Delhi areas. Our associate factories are currently approved for shipments to all US majors including Wal-Mart, Costco, Sam’s, JCP, Target, Kohl’s, Dkny, Sears, CJ Banks, PVH, CK, VF-Nautica, Saks Fifth, Macys, Bloomingdales Outlet, Gliks, Amazon, Bealls, BonTon and we are WRAP complied.

Men’s , Young Men’s , BLT

We do various products in both knits and wovens and do both tops and bottoms. We have huge exposure to fabric developments. Currently we work on slubs, jerseys, end on end, grindle, snow jersey, melanges, ribs, terry, fleece, jacquards, neps. In wovens we do YD qualities, chambrays, denims, twills, poplin prints, tencel and fancies.
